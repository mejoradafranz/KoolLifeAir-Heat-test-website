SIGNS YOU NEED NEW DUCTS

IS YOUR DUCT SYSTEM FAILING?

🌡️

Uneven Temperatures

Some rooms are too hot, others too cold — a clear sign that air isn't being distributed properly through your ducts.

💨

Weak Airflow

Low airflow from vents even when the AC is running hard usually points to duct leaks or crushed sections in the system.

💸

High Energy Bills

Leaky ducts force your HVAC to run longer to reach your set temperature — wasting energy and increasing your monthly costs.

🤧

Dust & Allergens

Cracks and gaps in ducts pull in attic dust, insulation particles, and allergens — circulating them throughout your home.

🔇

Rattling or Noise

Banging, rattling, or whistling from your vents can indicate loose, disconnected, or damaged duct sections.

📅

Old Duct System

Duct systems older than 15–20 years are often made with materials that degrade, sag, and leak — replacement is the smart move.

OUR PROCESS

HOW WE REPLACE YOUR DUCTS

We don't just swap old ducts for new ones — we redesign the system for your home's current layout and your HVAC equipment's output requirements.

01

HOME ASSESSMENT

We inspect your existing duct system, measure your home's square footage and layout, and evaluate your current HVAC equipment to design the right duct system.

02

CUSTOM DUCT DESIGN

We calculate proper duct sizing for every room — ensuring balanced airflow and maximum efficiency from your existing AC and heating units.

03

OLD DUCT REMOVAL

We carefully remove the old ductwork, clean the air handler cabinet, and prepare the system for the new installation.

04

NEW DUCT INSTALLATION

We install new ducts using quality materials, seal all connections with mastic or foil tape, and insulate properly for Orlando's climate.

05

AIRFLOW TESTING

We test airflow at every vent, verify the system is balanced, and confirm your HVAC is operating at full efficiency before we leave.
