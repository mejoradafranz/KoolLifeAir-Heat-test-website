OUR STORY

Founded in Orlando,
Built for Orlando

Kool Life Air & Heat was founded in 2014 by a Orlando native who understood firsthand how brutal the Texas summer can be — and how essential a reliable HVAC system is for every family's comfort and safety.

What started as a one-truck operation has grown into a full-service HVAC company with a team of NATE-certified technicians serving dozens of cities across the Orlando metro area. Through it all, we've never lost sight of what matters: showing up on time, doing the job right, and treating every customer like a neighbor.

We're proud to be a locally owned and operated business — every dollar you spend with us stays in the community we all share.
