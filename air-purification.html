WHAT WE REMOVE

CONTAMINANTS WE ELIMINATE

Orlando's sealed, air-conditioned environment traps a long list of pollutants inside your home. Our whole-home air purifiers tackle all of them at once.

🌸

Pollen & Allergens

Orlando's year-round allergen season means constant exposure to oak, cedar, ragweed, and grass pollen — major triggers for allergies and asthma attacks.

🐾

Pet Dander

Microscopic skin flakes from pets that trigger allergic reactions. They stay airborne for hours and settle on every surface — our systems trap them continuously.

🍄

Mold Spores

Orlando's humidity is a breeding ground for mold. Airborne spores cause respiratory issues and spread mold to new areas. Filtration stops them before they settle.

🦠

Bacteria & Viruses

Airborne pathogens that circulate through your HVAC system. Advanced purifiers with ionization or UV light neutralize them at the source.

💨

VOCs & Odors

Volatile organic compounds from cleaning products, paint, new furniture, and cooking. Activated carbon media filters absorb these chemical pollutants effectively.

🏭

Fine Particulate Matter

PM2.5 particles — smaller than a human hair — that penetrate deep into lungs. HEPA and media filters capture particles as small as 0.3 microns.

TYPES OF SYSTEMS

AIR PURIFIER OPTIONS

We install several types of whole-home air purification systems. Our technicians will recommend the best option based on your home's size, your specific concerns, and your budget.

🔬

Media Air Filters

High-efficiency media filters (MERV 11–16) replace your standard 1-inch filter with a deep, pleated media filter that captures particles 10–50x smaller than a standard filter. Low pressure drop, long filter life, and significant particle capture improvement — a great first step at any budget.

BEST VALUE
🌀

Electronic Air Cleaners

Electrostatic precipitators use an electrical charge to attract and capture airborne particles on collector plates. They capture extremely fine particles — including smoke and bacteria — and many models have washable cells that never need filter replacements. Ideal for pet owners and heavy dust environments.

MOST THOROUGH
⚡

Bipolar Ionization

Advanced systems like iWave emit positive and negative ions that attach to airborne particles, making them heavy enough to fall out of the air or be captured by your filter. Also neutralizes bacteria, viruses, and VOCs at the molecular level. Effective even between filter changes.

MOST ADVANCED
HOW THEY WORK TOGETHER

LAYERS OF PROTECTION

For maximum IAQ improvement, we often recommend combining a high-efficiency media filter with an ionization or UV system. Each layer captures different categories of pollutants — together they provide comprehensive protection.

  • Media filter handles particles: pollen, dust, dander, spores
  • UV light eliminates biological threats: bacteria, viruses, mold
  • Ionization tackles ultra-fine particles and odors
  • All installed in under 3 hours, no duct modification needed
