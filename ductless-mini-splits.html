WHAT IS A MINI-SPLIT?

POWERFUL COMFORT WITHOUT DUCTS

A ductless mini-split system has two main parts: a compact outdoor compressor unit and one or more sleek indoor air handlers mounted on the wall or ceiling. They're connected by a small refrigerant line — no ductwork required. The result is quiet, efficient, zone-specific comfort in any room, garage, or addition in your Orlando home.

THE BENEFITS

WHY GO DUCTLESS

⚡

Up to 30% More Efficient

Traditional ducted systems lose 20–30% of conditioned air through duct leaks and heat gain. Mini-splits deliver air directly to the space — no losses, lower bills.

🎯

Zone Control

Heat or cool individual rooms independently with separate thermostats. Stop paying to condition rooms nobody's using — a major advantage for Orlando homes with bonus rooms or home offices.

🔇

Ultra Quiet Operation

Indoor units operate at just 19–26 decibels — quieter than a whisper. No loud air rushing through ducts, no rattling grilles. You'll barely know it's running.

🏗️

No Ductwork Needed

Installation only requires a 3-inch hole through the wall for the refrigerant line. No demolition, no drywall repair, no weeks-long project. Most installs finish in one day.

🌡️

Heat & Cool Year-Round

Most mini-split systems are heat pumps, meaning they both cool in summer and heat in winter. One system, one installation, all-season comfort.

INSTALLATION PROCESS

INSTALLED IN ONE DAY

Most mini-split installations are completed in a single day with no major construction required. Here's what to expect:

01

FREE IN-HOME ESTIMATE

We assess the space, measure square footage, check insulation, and recommend the right BTU size and number of zones for your needs and budget.

02

INDOOR UNIT PLACEMENT

We mount the indoor air handler on the wall or ceiling in the optimal location for airflow coverage — typically high on an exterior wall.

03

OUTDOOR UNIT & LINE SET

We place the compressor outside, drill a 3-inch penetration through the wall, and run the refrigerant and electrical line set through a small conduit.

04

CHARGE, TEST & WALK-THROUGH

We charge the system with refrigerant, test all modes and zones, and walk you through the remote control and smart thermostat options before we leave.
