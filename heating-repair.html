COMMON PROBLEMS WE FIX

IS YOUR HEATER DOING ANY OF THIS?

Central Florida winters are short but can hit hard — especially when a cold front drops temperatures 40 degrees overnight. A heater that's been sitting dormant all summer is exactly the kind that breaks when you need it most. Here are the most common heating problems we fix every day.

❄️

No Heat Coming Out

Furnace running but blowing cold air — usually a failed heat exchanger, bad ignitor, faulty gas valve, or tripped limit switch. We diagnose it fast and carry most parts on the truck.

💨

Heater Won't Turn On

Could be a thermostat issue, tripped breaker, failed ignition board, pilot light out, or a clogged condensate drain tripping the safety float switch. We trace the fault quickly.

🔊

Banging or Rattling

Loose access panels, dirty burners causing delayed ignition "boom," or a failing blower motor bearing. Unusual sounds mean something is stressed — don't ignore them.

👃

Burning or Gas Smell

A gas smell is a safety emergency. Leave the house, don't operate any switches, and call us and your gas company immediately. A burning dust smell on first use is normal — anything else is not.

🔄

Short Cycling

Heater turning on and off repeatedly every few minutes. Often caused by a dirty air filter causing overheating, a faulty thermostat, or a heat exchanger safety limit tripping on high temperature.

💸

High Energy Bills

A heating system that's dirty, mis-calibrated, or losing efficiency works harder and costs more. Annual tune-ups and timely repairs keep your system running at peak efficiency.

REPAIR OR REPLACE?

WE'LL GIVE YOU AN HONEST ANSWER

We don't push replacements to earn a bigger ticket. If your heater is worth repairing, we'll repair it. If it's costing you more than a new system would, we'll tell you that too — and give you a fair quote either way.

REPAIR IS USUALLY WORTH IT WHEN:
  • Your system is under 10 years old
  • The repair cost is under 50% of replacement cost
  • It's a single component failure (ignitor, capacitor, valve)
  • No history of repeated breakdowns
REPLACEMENT MAKES SENSE WHEN:
  • Your system is 15+ years old
  • You've had multiple repairs in the past couple of years
  • The heat exchanger is cracked (safety hazard — replace immediately)
  • Your energy bills keep rising despite repairs
⚠️ CRACKED HEAT EXCHANGER — DON'T IGNORE IT

A cracked heat exchanger is a serious safety hazard. It can allow combustion gases — including carbon monoxide — to enter your home's air supply. If your technician finds a cracked heat exchanger, do not continue operating the furnace. Call us and we'll walk you through your options safely and honestly.
