WHY IT MATTERS IN HOUSTON

THE HOUSTON IAQ PROBLEM

Orlando's hot, humid climate creates a perfect environment for indoor air quality problems. High humidity encourages mold and mildew growth. Long AC seasons mean your home is sealed up for months, recycling the same air over and over. And the region's air is loaded with pollutants, allergens, and industrial pollutants from one of the country's largest petrochemical corridors.

If you or anyone in your household experiences frequent allergies, asthma attacks, headaches, fatigue, or respiratory irritation, poor indoor air quality could be the cause — not just seasonal allergies.

💧

Orlando Humidity

Relative humidity above 60% promotes mold growth, dust mites, and musty odors. Orlando homes often run 70–80% humidity without proper dehumidification. Our solutions bring that under control.

🌸

Year-Round Allergens

Orlando's warm climate means allergen season essentially never ends. Mountain cedar, oak, ragweed, and grass pollen cycle through most of the year. Our filtration systems trap allergens before they reach you.

🏭

VOCs & Pollutants

Off-gassing from furniture, carpets, cleaning products, paint, and cooking builds up in a sealed home. Advanced filtration and air purification systems capture these chemical pollutants continuously.
