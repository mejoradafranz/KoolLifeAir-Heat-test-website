SERVICE
How fast can you come out?
We offer same-day service for most calls booked before noon, and we operate 24/7 for true emergencies. During peak summer months, we add extra technicians to keep wait times short. Most customers see us within 2–4 hours of calling.
SERVICE
What areas do you serve?
We serve the Greater Houston metro area including Houston, Katy, Sugar Land, The Woodlands, Pearland, Pasadena, League City, Friendswood, Missouri City, Spring, Cypress, Humble, Conroe, Baytown, Deer Park, and surrounding communities within about 50 miles.
FINANCING
Do you offer financing?
Yes — we offer flexible financing options including 0% interest for 12 months and low monthly payment plans for qualified customers. Applications take about 5 minutes and most approvals are instant. Visit our Financing page for details.
INSTALLATION
What brands do you carry?
We work with all major HVAC brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Goodman, Daikin, Rheem, and more. During your free estimate, we'll recommend the best brand and model for your home's size, layout, and budget — with no pressure.
INSTALLATION
How long does installation take?
Most standard AC system replacements take 4–6 hours from start to finish. More complex installations involving new ductwork or zoning systems can take a full day. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your estimate so you can plan accordingly.
MAINTENANCE
What's included in a maintenance visit?
Our full tune-up includes a 21-point inspection: cleaning coils, checking refrigerant levels, inspecting electrical connections, lubricating moving parts, testing thermostat accuracy, replacing the filter, and providing a written system health report. Members of our Comfort Club get priority scheduling and discounts too.
WARRANTY
Do you offer warranties?
Absolutely. All new equipment comes with the manufacturer's warranty (typically 5–10 years on parts). We also provide a 1-year labor warranty on all installations and a 30-day parts and labor warranty on all repairs. If something goes wrong, we make it right.
REPAIRS
What if my system breaks down after a repair?
If the same issue recurs within 30 days of a repair, we'll come back and fix it at no additional charge. We stand behind our work completely. Call us immediately and we'll treat it as a priority service call.
SERVICE
Do you service all HVAC brands?
Yes. Our NATE-certified technicians are trained to work on all major residential and commercial HVAC brands. Whether you have an older Rheem unit or a brand-new Lennox, we have the tools, parts, and knowledge to diagnose and repair it correctly.
COSTS
How much does a new AC system cost?
Costs vary based on the size of your home, the efficiency rating you choose, and any ductwork modifications needed. A basic replacement typically ranges from $3,500–$6,000. High-efficiency or larger systems can run $7,000–$12,000+. We provide free, no-obligation estimates so you know exactly what to expect.
INSTALLATION
What SEER rating should I choose?
In Houston's hot climate, we generally recommend a minimum of 16 SEER2 for new installations. Higher SEER ratings (18–20+) cost more upfront but significantly lower your monthly energy bills. We'll calculate the payback period for each option so you can make an informed decision.
MAINTENANCE
How often should I change my filter?
For standard 1-inch filters, every 30–60 days is ideal for Houston homes, especially during peak cooling season. Thicker 4-inch media filters can go 3–6 months. Homes with pets or allergy sufferers should change more frequently. We'll remind you when it's time if you're on our maintenance plan.
