WHY IT MATTERS

TOO MUCH HUMIDITY IS A REAL PROBLEM IN ORLANDO

Florida consistently ranks among the most humid states in the country. When indoor humidity exceeds 60%, your home becomes a breeding ground for mold, mildew, dust mites, and allergens — even if your AC is running perfectly.

Portable dehumidifiers only treat one room at a time and require constant emptying. A whole-house dehumidifier works automatically with your existing HVAC system to maintain ideal humidity levels throughout your entire home — without you lifting a finger.

WARNING SIGNS

SIGNS YOUR HOME NEEDS A
WHOLE-HOUSE DEHUMIDIFIER

If you notice any of these in your Orlando home, excess humidity may be to blame.

🤧

Allergy & Asthma Flare-Ups

High humidity encourages dust mites and mold spores, which are the top triggers for respiratory issues indoors.

🍄

Musty Odors or Visible Mold

A persistent musty smell — especially in bathrooms, closets, or the garage — signals active mold or mildew growth.

💦

Condensation on Windows

Water droplets forming on your interior windows are a classic sign that indoor humidity is too high.

🪵

Warping Wood or Flooring

Wood floors, cabinets, and furniture expand and warp when exposed to excess moisture over time.

🥵

Feeling Sticky & Uncomfortable

If your home still feels clammy even with the AC running, moisture in the air is outpacing your system's ability to remove it.

⚡

High Energy Bills

When humidity is high, your AC works harder and longer to make the space feel comfortable, driving up your monthly energy costs.
