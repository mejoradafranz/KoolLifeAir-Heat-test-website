COMMON QUESTIONS

Financing FAQ

Does applying affect my credit score? +
The initial pre-qualification uses a soft credit inquiry, which does not affect your score. A hard inquiry only occurs when you accept a financing offer and formally apply.
What credit score do I need to qualify? +
We work with multiple lenders to serve a wide range of credit profiles. While better rates are available to those with scores above 680, many customers with lower scores still qualify for financing options.
Can I finance a repair, or only installations? +
Financing is available for both repairs and new system installations. There is a minimum job total required — ask our team for current thresholds when you call.
How do I make my monthly payments? +
Payments are made directly to our financing partner, not to Kool Life. You'll receive login credentials to manage your account online, set up autopay, and view statements at any time.
